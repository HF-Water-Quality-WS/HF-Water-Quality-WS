4th International Workshop on High Temporal Resolution Water Quality Monitoring and Analysis

31st May to 2nd June 2022

Swedish University of Agricultural Sciences, Uppsala, Sweden (Online)

The 2022 edition of the International Workshop on High Temporal Resolution Water Quality Monitoring and Analysis was hosted online by the Swedish University of Agricultural Sciences, Uppsala, Sweden.
The workshop was held over 3 days and consisted of 3 keynote lectures, 36 contributed oral presentations nd 3 discussion sessions.
Themes of the workshop centred around the 3 keynote lectures:

  • New advances in high-resolution water quality monitoring - Prof. James Kirchner.
  • Extracting hydrochemical patterns and modelling of high-resolution data - Prof. Nandita Basu.
  • Understanding hydrochemical and biogeochemical processes from high-resolution data - Prof. Helen Jarvie.
